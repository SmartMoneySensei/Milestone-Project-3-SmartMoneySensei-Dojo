In forex, Fundamental Analysis is a method of reviewing market News & Value by examining related economic and financial factors. Fundamental analysts study anything that can affect the forex market, from macroeconomic factors such as the state of the economy and industry conditions to microeconomic factors like the effectiveness of the corporations impact.

Supply and Demand determines price, or in our case, the currency exchange rate. Using supply and demand as an indicator of where price could be headed is easy. The hard part is analyzing all of the factors that affect supply and demand.

The idea behind Fundamental Analysis is that if a country's current or future economic outlook is good, its currency should strengthen. The better shape a country's economy is, the more foreign businesses and investors will invest in that country.
