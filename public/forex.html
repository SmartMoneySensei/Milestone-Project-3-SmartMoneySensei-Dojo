What is Forex⁉️

~ Foreign Exchange (forex or FX) is the trading of one currency for another. Transactions take place in the foreign exchange market, also known as the forex market.

For example: Imagine taking a trip to Europe. You could Swap $100 U.S. dollar for 100 Euro. If the Euro value increases 2 times its' original value, then swapping back to USD will result in $200 with an ROI of $100. 🏦
