In forex, Sentimental Analysis can be a useful tool to help traders understand and act on price behavior. While applying sound Technical and Fundamental Analysis is key, having an additional feel for the market consensus can add depth to a trader's view of forex and other markets.

Sentimental Analysis is used to gauge how other traders feel, whether it's about the overall currency market or about a particular currency pair.

In conclusion, Sentimental Analysis is the idea that each trader has his or her own opinion of why the market is acting the way it does and whether to trade in the same direction of the market or against it.
